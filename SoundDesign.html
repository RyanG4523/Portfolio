SFX Narrative

The goal of this roject was to create a narrative that was only told through sound with little to no dialogue. For this project I decided to try to tell the story of a soldier in a battle finding an object magic in nature and is cursed by it. He was able to single handedly turn the tide of battle but is horrorified by what he has become.
